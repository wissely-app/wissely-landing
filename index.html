Questions

Things people
ask us.

Do I need accounting knowledge?

No. Wissely is built for business owners who are not accountants. Plain English answers, no financial jargon anywhere on the platform.

Is my financial data safe?

Wissely is designed with privacy and security in mind. Please review our Data & Security page for current information regarding data handling and privacy practices.

Can I cancel anytime?

You can cancel your subscription at any time. No contracts or cancellation fees. We also send a reminder before your free trial ends.

What can I paste into Wissely?

Anything text based. Invoice details, expense lists, bank statement summaries, financial notes. PDF upload is coming in Phase 2.

How is this different from QuickBooks?

QuickBooks focuses on bookkeeping and accounting workflows. Wissely focuses on explaining financial information in plain English.

Is the free trial really free?

Completely free. No credit card required. You get 20 analyses and full feature access for 14 days, and only pay if you decide to continue after that.
